How to install the extension

Install ZAS Safeguard from the Chrome Web Store in under a minute.

  1. Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for "ZAS Safeguard"
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" on the ZAS Safeguard listing
  3. Click "Add extension" in the confirmation popup
  4. The ZAS Safeguard icon will appear in your browser toolbar
  5. Click the icon and log in to your account — or create one if you do not have one yet

The extension starts protecting you immediately after you log in.

How to log in to the dashboard

Access your dashboard at zassafeguard.com/app/dashboard.

  1. Go to zassafeguard.com and click Sign In
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. You will be taken to your dashboard automatically

If you signed up with Google, click "Continue with Google" instead of entering a password.

How to set up parental controls

Add a child device and configure rules from the Family section.

  1. Open the dashboard and go to the Family section
  2. Click "Add Child Profile" and enter the child's name
  3. Install ZAS Safeguard on the child's device and log in using the same account
  4. In the dashboard, go to Devices and link the new device to the child's profile
  5. Adjust blocked categories for that profile under Protection settings

What the dashboard shows

An overview of your protection status, stats, and connected devices.

The dashboard shows: how many ads and trackers were blocked today, how many sites were blocked, how many devices are connected, and any security alerts that need your attention. The status card at the top shows whether all protection layers are active. Quick Actions let you start Focus Mode, lock internet access, or scan a link from one place.

How to change your password

Update your password from the Settings page.

  1. Go to Settings in the dashboard sidebar
  2. Under Account, click Change Password
  3. Enter your current password and the new one
  4. Click Save

How to manage connected devices

View, rename, and remove devices from the Devices section.

Go to the Devices section in the sidebar. You will see all devices that have ZAS Safeguard installed and are linked to your account. Click any device to rename it or remove it. Removing a device does not uninstall the extension — it just unlinks it from your account.

Extension is not connecting to the dashboard

The most common fix is to log out and log back in.

  1. Click the ZAS Safeguard icon in your toolbar
  2. Click Log Out
  3. Go to zassafeguard.com/login and log back in
  4. The extension will reconnect automatically after login

If this does not work: try removing the extension and reinstalling it from the Chrome Web Store.

Stats are not showing in the dashboard

Stats sync every 30 seconds when the extension is active.

The dashboard reads stats from both the extension directly and from Firestore logs. If stats show zero, make sure the extension is installed and you are logged in on the same account. Stats that happened before you installed the extension will not appear retroactively.

I accidentally blocked a site I need

Add it to your custom allowlist from the Ad Blocker section.

  1. Go to the Ad Blocker section in the dashboard
  2. Under Allowlist, click Add Domain
  3. Enter the domain you want to allow (e.g. example.com)
  4. The site will be unblocked on all your connected devices within a few seconds

YouTube ads are not being blocked

YouTube requires the Pro plan and a specific setup.

YouTube ad blocking requires a Pro subscription. If you have Pro and YouTube ads are still showing: make sure the extension is active on youtube.com (check the toolbar icon), try refreshing the page, and make sure you are not using a VPN which can interfere with ad blocking. YouTube frequently updates its ad delivery — if blocking stops working we push an update quickly.

How to cancel your subscription

Cancel from Settings with one click. No penalty.

  1. Go to Settings in the dashboard
  2. Under Billing, click Manage Subscription
  3. Click Cancel Subscription
  4. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period

Adult content blocking stays active forever even after cancellation.

What happens when my trial ends

You are charged the plan rate unless you cancel first.

We send a reminder email 24 hours before your trial expires. If you do not cancel, you are automatically charged for the plan you signed up for. If you cancel before the trial ends, you will not be charged anything. To cancel: go to Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription.

How to update your payment method

Update payment details from the billing portal.

  1. Go to Settings in the dashboard
  2. Under Billing, click Manage Subscription
  3. You will be taken to the Stripe billing portal
  4. Under Payment Methods, add or update your card
