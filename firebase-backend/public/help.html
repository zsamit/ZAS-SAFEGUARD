How to install the extension

Install ZAS Safeguard from the Chrome Web Store in under a minute.

  1. Search "ZAS Safeguard" in the Chrome Web Store
  2. Click "Add to Chrome" on the listing
  3. Click "Add extension" in the confirmation popup
  4. The ZAS icon will appear in your toolbar
  5. Click the icon and log in to your account

Protection starts immediately after login.

How to access the dashboard

Go to zassafeguard.com and click Sign In.

  1. Go to zassafeguard.com and click Sign In
  2. Enter your email and password
  3. You are taken to your dashboard automatically

If you used Google sign-up, click "Continue with Google" instead.

How to set up parental controls

Add a child device and configure rules from the Family section.

  1. Open the dashboard and go to Family
  2. Click "Add Child Profile" and enter the child's name
  3. Install ZAS Safeguard on the child's device and log in with the same account
  4. In the dashboard, go to Devices and link the device to the child's profile
  5. Adjust blocked categories for that profile under Protection

What the dashboard shows

Protection status, stats, alerts, and connected devices — all in one place.

The status card at the top shows whether all protection layers are active. Stats show ads blocked, sites blocked, and devices online today. The Alerts section flags anything that needs your attention. Quick Actions let you start Focus Mode or Internet Lock with one click.

How to change your password

Update your password from Settings.

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar
  2. Under Account, click Change Password
  3. Enter your current password and new password
  4. Click Save

How to manage connected devices

View, rename, and remove devices from the Devices section.

Go to Devices in the sidebar. Click any device to rename or remove it. Removing a device does not uninstall the extension — it unlinks it from your account.

Extension not connecting to dashboard

Log out and back in — fixes it almost every time.

  1. Click the ZAS icon in your toolbar
  2. Click Log Out
  3. Go to zassafeguard.com/login and sign in again
  4. The extension reconnects automatically

If this does not work, remove and reinstall the extension from the Chrome Web Store.

Stats not showing in dashboard

Stats sync every 30 seconds when the extension is active.

If stats show zero, make sure the extension is installed and you are signed in with the same account as the dashboard. Stats do not backfill — only events after install appear.

Accidentally blocked a site I need

Add it to your allowlist from the Ad Blocker section.

  1. Go to Ad Blocker in the sidebar
  2. Under Allowlist, click Add Domain
  3. Enter the domain (e.g. example.com)
  4. The site becomes accessible within seconds across all devices

YouTube ads still showing

YouTube ad blocking requires a Pro subscription and specific conditions.

YouTube ad blocking requires Pro. If you have Pro and ads still show: verify the extension is active on youtube.com (check toolbar icon), try refreshing the tab, and make sure you are not using a VPN. YouTube updates its ad delivery frequently — we push extension updates quickly when blocking breaks.

How to cancel your subscription

Cancel from Settings with one click. Access continues until period ends.

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar
  2. Under Billing, click Manage Subscription
  3. Click Cancel Subscription
  4. Your access continues until the end of the billing period

Adult content blocking stays on permanently after cancellation.

What happens when my trial ends

You are charged unless you cancel before the trial expires.

We send a reminder email 24 hours before your trial expires. If you do not cancel, you are automatically charged for the plan you selected. To cancel: Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription.

How to update payment method

Update from the Stripe billing portal via Settings.

  1. Go to Settings in the sidebar
  2. Under Billing, click Manage Subscription
  3. You are taken to the Stripe billing portal
  4. Under Payment Methods, add or update your card
