The internet should protect you.
Not the other way around.

We built ZAS Safeguard because the tools that existed were either too complicated, too expensive, or too easy to bypass. So we built something simpler and harder to get around.

The problem we are solving

Every parent who has ever handed a child a device knows the feeling. The internet contains things that no child should see — and the default settings on every browser, every platform, and every search engine are set to allow almost everything.

The existing parental control tools are expensive, require separate accounts, break constantly, and are often bypassed by any teenager who spends five minutes on YouTube. The free alternatives are either outdated blocklists that miss most modern adult sites, or surveillance tools that feel invasive rather than protective.

We also built this for adults who want to protect themselves. Who recognise that certain content is harmful to their own mental health, their relationships, or their productivity — and who want a tool that actually helps, not one they can easily disable when willpower fails.

What we believe

01

Protection should be unconditional

Adult content blocking is always on. Not toggleable. Not bypassable with a simple click. If you built protection that turns off when someone really wants to turn it off, you built nothing.

02

Simplicity is a form of respect

You should not need a technical degree to protect your family online. One extension. One dashboard. Real protection working in the background without you thinking about it.

03

Privacy is not optional

We do not sell your browsing data. We do not build profiles. We do not serve ads. Your protection data is used only to protect you — nothing else.
