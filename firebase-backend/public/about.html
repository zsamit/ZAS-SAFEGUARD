The internet should protect you.
Not the other way around.

We built ZAS Safeguard because the tools that existed were either too complicated, too expensive, or too easy to bypass. So we built something simpler — and harder to circumvent.

The problem we are solving

Every parent who has ever handed a child a device knows the feeling. The internet contains things no child should see — and the default settings on every browser and platform are configured to allow almost everything.

Existing parental control tools are expensive, break constantly, require separate accounts, and are bypassed by any teenager who spends five minutes on YouTube. The free alternatives are outdated blocklists that miss most modern adult sites, or surveillance tools that feel invasive rather than protective.

We also built this for adults who want to protect themselves. Who recognise that certain content is harmful to their mental health, their relationships, or their productivity — and who want a tool that actually helps, not one they can disable when willpower fails.

What we believe

01

Protection should be unconditional

Adult content blocking is always on. Not toggleable. Not bypassable with one click. If you built protection that turns off when someone really wants it off, you built nothing.

02

Simplicity is a form of respect

You should not need a technical degree to protect your family online. One extension. One dashboard. Real protection working in the background without you thinking about it.

03

Privacy is non-negotiable

We do not sell your browsing data. We do not build profiles. We do not serve ads. Your data is used only to protect you — nothing else, ever.
