Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

ZAS Safeguard uses minimal browser storage to make the product work. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

What is stored and why

  • Firebase auth token — stored in localStorage when you log in. Expires every hour, auto-refreshed while active. Deleted on logout.
  • Extension ID — a string so the dashboard can talk to your installed extension. Not a tracking identifier.
  • User preferences — stored in your account on our servers, not in a cookie.
  • Stripe session cookie — set by Stripe during payment processing only. Deleted after payment completes. Governed by Stripe's privacy policy.

What we do NOT store

  • No advertising or tracking cookies
  • No third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Facebook Pixel)
  • No cross-site tracking or fingerprinting of any kind

How to clear stored data

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  • Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Clearing data will log you out until you sign in again.
