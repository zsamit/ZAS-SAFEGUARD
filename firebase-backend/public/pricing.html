Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel any time?
+
Yes. Cancel from your account settings at any time. No questions asked. If you cancel during a billing period your access continues until the end of that period.
What happens when my trial ends?
+
You will be charged the plan rate you selected unless you cancel before the trial ends. We send a reminder email 24 hours before the trial expires. Adult content blocking always remains active even if you do not subscribe.
Does it work on Chrome, Firefox, and Safari?
+
ZAS Safeguard works on Chrome and all Chromium-based browsers (Edge, Brave, Opera). Firefox support is in development. Safari support for iOS and macOS is planned.
What is the difference between Free and Pro?
+
Free includes permanent adult content blocking. Pro adds everything else: real-time threat detection, ad and tracker removal, AI content analysis, Focus Mode, Internet Lock, the Family Dashboard, and parent email alerts.
Is my browsing data stored?
+
We store only the information needed to provide the service — blocked site logs (domain only, no full URL), alert events, and device identifiers. We do not sell this data, share it with advertisers, or build profiles from it.
Can one account protect multiple devices?
+
Yes. Pro covers unlimited devices. Install the extension on every device in your household and manage them all from one dashboard.
Can I change my plan after subscribing?
+
Yes. You can switch between monthly and yearly from your account settings at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
