Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel any time?
+
Yes. Cancel from account settings at any time. No questions, no penalty. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
What happens when my trial ends?
+
You will be charged the rate of the plan you selected unless you cancel before the trial ends. We send a reminder email 24 hours before expiry. Adult content blocking stays on permanently regardless.
What browsers are supported?
+
Chrome and all Chromium-based browsers — Edge, Brave, Opera. Firefox support is in development. Safari planned.
Free vs Pro — what is the real difference?
+
Free gives you permanent adult blocking. Pro adds real-time threat detection, ad and tracker removal, AI content analysis, Focus Mode, Internet Lock, the Family Dashboard for unlimited devices, and email alerts for parents.
Is my browsing data stored?
+
We store only what is needed to run the service — blocked site domain logs, alert events, and device identifiers. We do not sell this data or build profiles from it.
How many devices does one account cover?
+
Pro covers unlimited devices. Install on every device in your household and manage from one dashboard.
Can I switch between monthly and yearly?
+
Yes, from account settings at any time. Upgrades are immediate. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
