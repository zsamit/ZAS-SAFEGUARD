8 layers of protection

Everything ZAS Safeguard does

One extension. Eight intelligent layers working together to keep you and your family safe online.

Always Active

Adult Content Blocking

Blocks explicit content, adult websites, and inappropriate material at the network level — before it ever reaches your screen. This protection is always on and cannot be disabled, even if the extension is temporarily toggled off.

Uses a continuously updated blocklist of millions of domains combined with AI-powered URL pattern matching for sites too new to be listed.

For parents & self-protection
Real-time

Phishing & Malware Protection

Every URL you navigate to is scanned in under 50ms against our threat intelligence database. Phishing sites, malware distributors, scam pages, and deceptive redirect chains are blocked before they load.

Powered by Google Safe Browsing, our own threat database, and AI pattern recognition trained on millions of malicious URLs.

For everyone
Pro

Ad & Tracker Removal

Removes intrusive ads from every page you visit — banners, pop-ups, video pre-rolls, autoplay ads. Simultaneously blocks over 200 known trackers from following your browsing activity across sites.

Pages load faster and use less bandwidth. Your browsing history stays yours.

For privacy-conscious users
Pro · AI

AI Content Analysis

When keyword matching is not enough, our AI analyses page content in real time to detect harmful material that slips through traditional blocklists — including new sites, reworded content, and context-dependent material.

Uses GPT-4o-mini for content classification. Analysis runs server-side so your browsing data never leaves securely.
